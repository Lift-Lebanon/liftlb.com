Who we are

A Lebanese medical device establishment, building a distribution business around implants that hospitals and clinics can rely on.

LIFT is a Lebanese medical device establishment specializing in the distribution of dental and orthopedic implants and related surgical products to hospitals, orthopedic surgeons and dental clinics across Lebanon.

The establishment is formally registered under Commercial Register No. 2801282 and is registered with the Lebanese Ministry of Finance. We are establishing our supply partnerships and completing Ministry of Public Health product registration ahead of our first import.

Our operating standard will be full unit traceability, documented compliance and transparent reporting — the standard we believe medical device distribution in Lebanon should be held to, for every partner we work with.

How we operate

The commitments we hold ourselves to, from the moment a shipment lands to the moment an implant reaches a patient.

Every unit is traceable

Each implant will be tracked by lot and serial number from arrival through to the patient. It is a legal requirement in Lebanon, and we treat it as fundamental rather than administrative.

Registered before it is offered

Every product will be registered with the Ministry of Public Health before it is offered for sale in Lebanon.

Manufacturer certification

We require CE marking, ISO 13485 and ISO 10993 documentation from every supplier we work with.

Stock held, not ordered on demand

We will carry a working range of sizes rather than ordering case by case, so the system a surgeon specifies is available when it is chosen.

Expiry under control

Every unit will carry a recorded expiry date and be monitored continuously, with advance alerts before shelf life is reached.

Documented operations

Purchase orders, shipments, inventory, consignment and sales will be recorded in a single controlled system.

03

Working with partners

What a manufacturer — or the distributor already representing one in Lebanon — can expect from LIFT.

Close to the clinic

LIFT is built to sell directly to the surgeons and clinics who specify implants, rather than waiting for orders to come to us. Our approach is to be present in the clinic, not at the end of a supply chain.

Regulatory handling

We handle MoPH product registration and customs clearance in full before any product reaches the Lebanese market.

Reporting on request

Every sale, consignment and stock position will be recorded unit by unit, and reported on request.

For manufacturers

We are open to exclusive or semi-exclusive distribution agreements for Lebanon, with agreed volumes and territory.

For local distributors

Where a manufacturer is already represented in Lebanon, we are open to working under the existing partner — as a sub-distributor, dealer, or covering a defined region or product line. Our aim is to extend reach, not to compete for it.

Long-term intent

Our plan builds from dental implants into orthopedics over three years — we are building relationships meant to last that long.
