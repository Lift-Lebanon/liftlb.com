Who we are

A Lebanese medical device company, building a distribution business around implants that hospitals and clinics can rely on.

LIFT Establishment is a Lebanese medical device company specialising in the distribution of dental and orthopedic implants and related surgical products to hospitals, orthopedic surgeons and dental clinics across Lebanon.

The company is formally registered under Commercial Register No. 2801282 and is registered with the Lebanese Ministry of Finance. We are establishing our supply partnerships and completing Ministry of Public Health product registration ahead of our first import.

Our operating standard is full unit traceability, documented compliance and transparent reporting — the standard we believe medical device distribution in Lebanon should be held to, for every partner we work with.

How we operate

An implant that arrives late, or cannot be traced, is a problem for everyone in the room. These are the commitments we hold ourselves to.

Every unit is traceable

Each implant is tracked by lot and serial number from arrival through to the patient. It is a legal requirement in Lebanon, and we treat it as fundamental rather than administrative.

Registered before it is offered

No product is offered for sale before it is registered with the Ministry of Public Health. We require CE marking and ISO 13485 documentation from every manufacturer we work with.

Stock held, not ordered on demand

We will carry a working range of sizes rather than ordering case by case, so the system a surgeon specifies is available when it is chosen.

Consignment where it helps

For hospitals that need implants on hand, our model places stock on site and invoices on use — with every unit accounted for and retrieved before it reaches its expiry.

Working with manufacturers

What a manufacturer can expect from LIFT as a partner in Lebanon.

Close to the clinic

LIFT is built to sell directly to the surgeons and clinics who specify implants, rather than waiting for orders to come to us. Our approach is to be present in the clinic, not at the end of a supply chain.

Regulatory handling

We handle MoPH product registration and customs clearance in full before any product reaches the Lebanese market.

Reporting on request

Every sale, consignment and stock position will be recorded unit by unit, and reported on request.

Long-term intent

Our plan builds from dental implants into orthopedics over three years — we are building relationships meant to last that long.
