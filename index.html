02

Who we serve

Our target segments, and the model we will serve them with.

Hospitals

Private and university hospitals in Beirut, Mount Lebanon and the North. Our model will be direct supply, with consignment stock held on site.

Orthopedic surgeons

Specification-led relationships. The surgeon selects the implant system; we will ensure availability, sizing range and on-time delivery.

Dental clinics

Our launch segment. Our model will be direct supply of implant systems with the size range clinics need, held in stock rather than ordered case by case.

Consignment model

We will place stock on site so the surgeon is never short mid-procedure, and will invoice only on use — tracked unit by unit.

Why Lebanon needs reliable supply
