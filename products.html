What we require of a manufacturer

The documentation we ask for before a product is listed or offered in Lebanon.

CE marking

Current CE certification for every product line we carry, with the notified body identified.

ISO 13485 and ISO 10993

Quality management certification for the manufacturing site, and biological evaluation documentation for the materials used.

Full size range

A complete diameter and length range. A partial range means the surgeon chooses another system, and the account is lost.

Lot and serial data

Lot and serial numbers on every unit, so traceability from arrival to patient can be maintained without manual reconstruction.
